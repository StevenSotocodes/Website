About

Hi! I'm Pete Thinkful

I'm an artist living in Denver, Colorado.

As an artist, I'm interested in:

  • Producing abstract art
  • Creating great graffiti
  • Connecting with like-minded artists

If you're looking to learn more, feel free to contact me.

Pete’s Background

After graduating college, I became an art teacher and worked with beginners and professionals. I've won awards and my work has been featured in major art galleries across the U.S.

If you're looking to hire an artist or if you're an artist looking to collaborate on your next project, please reach out! I'm excited to work with other artists to create new art.
